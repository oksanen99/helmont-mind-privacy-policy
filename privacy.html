Privacy Policy for Helmont Mind

Last updated: April 15, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Helmont Mind collects only the information necessary to provide its services:

  • Google account email address (for Google Calendar sync)
  • Google Calendar event data (read and write access)
  • App data stored locally on your device (tasks, notes, alarms, reminders)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Google Calendar data is used solely to sync your calendar events with the app.
  • All personal data (tasks, notes, alarms) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • AI assistant queries are processed via our secure backend proxy. No personal identifying information is sent to AI providers.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Google Calendar data is accessed only on your behalf using Google's official APIs.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Calendar data: stored locally on your device via Google's secure OAuth 2.0 authentication.
  • App data: stored locally using Android's secure storage (SharedPreferences).

5. Third-Party Services

This app uses:

  • Google Calendar API (subject to Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Anthropic Claude API for AI features (queries only, no personal data)

6. Your Rights

You can revoke Google Calendar access at any time via your Google Account settings (myaccount.google.com → Security → Third-party access).

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: service@helmont.fi

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
